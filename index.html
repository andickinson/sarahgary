Tea Ceremony & Bidding

Traditionally, The Bidding was the delivery of the bride’s dowry by the groom and his family. In modern times, The Bidding is a bit of fun whereby the groom (with the assistance of his groomsman) will pass some ‘trials’ in order to enter the location of the Tea Ceremony where his bride awaits. These trials are set by the bridesmaids, and often combine traditional elements to bless the couple, with modern day games.

A Tea Ceremony is a traditional event held before a wedding ceremony, which presents the soon-to-be wedded couple to significant elders they respect in their lives. The couple present each guest with a cup of tea, to thank them for their assistance and teachings through their life so far. Guests will often give the empty tea-cup back with a small gift inside a red packet.

Guests will have been informed if they have been invited to Sarah & Gary’s Tea Ceremony. Dress code for this event is cocktail, and substantial finger food will be provided for dinner.
